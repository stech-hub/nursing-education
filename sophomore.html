Interactive Q&A

Q1: What is pathophysiology?

Pathophysiology is the study of functional changes in the body caused by disease or injury.

Q2: Define advanced pharmacology.

It is the study of drug actions, interactions, and effects on various body systems.

Q3: What is the purpose of health assessment?

To evaluate the patient's physical, emotional, and psychological status.

Q4: Name two common infection control methods.

Hand hygiene and proper disposal of medical waste.

Q5: Why are care plans important?

They guide nursing interventions and ensure consistent patient care.
