Notes & Modules

Medical-Surgical Nursing II: Advanced patient care for complex conditions, surgical interventions, ICU basics, and critical thinking.

Pharmacology II: Advanced drug therapy, interactions, emergency medications, dosage calculations, and patient monitoring.

Advanced Pathophysiology: Mechanisms of chronic diseases, organ dysfunctions, and patient care implications.

Pediatric Nursing II: Complex pediatric conditions, growth monitoring, vaccination, family support.

Maternal & Child Health II: High-risk pregnancies, labor complications, newborn emergencies, postpartum management.

Community Health Nursing: Public health, epidemiology, health promotion, disease prevention, home visits, and outreach.

Procedures: Advanced IV therapy, catheter care, tracheostomy, wound dressing, patient assessment, monitoring devices, and emergency care.

Advanced patient care for complex conditions, surgical interventions, and critical thinking.
