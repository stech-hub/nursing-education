Interactive Q&A

Q1: Define critical care nursing.

Critical care nursing involves caring for patients with life-threatening conditions requiring close monitoring and specialized interventions.

Q2: What is evidence-based practice?

Evidence-based practice uses current best evidence, clinical expertise, and patient preferences to make decisions about patient care.

Q3: Why is leadership important in nursing?

Leadership ensures effective team coordination, quality care, and efficient resource management.

Q4: Name two emergency nursing skills.

CPR and rapid assessment of trauma patients.

Q5: What is quality improvement in nursing?

Processes that enhance patient care, safety, and clinical outcomes through systematic review and changes.
