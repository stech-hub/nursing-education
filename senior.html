Notes & Modules

Advanced Nursing Practice: Specialized care, advanced assessment, critical thinking, patient-centered interventions, and complex case management.

Nursing Leadership & Management: Team leadership, decision-making, delegation, quality improvement, policy development, and conflict resolution.

Nursing Research Methods: Research designs, data collection, analysis, evidence-based practice, and implementing research findings.

Internship / Clinical Practice: Hands-on experience in hospitals, patient care under supervision, documentation, and professional growth.

Community & Public Health Advanced Concepts: Health promotion strategies, epidemiology, disaster management, and community project planning.

Sample Q&A

Q1: What is advanced nursing practice?

A: It is specialized, evidence-based nursing care for complex patient situations, requiring advanced knowledge and skills.

Q2: Name two key responsibilities of a nurse leader.

A: Team management and policy implementation for quality patient care.

Q3: Why is research important in nursing?

A: Research provides evidence for best practices, improves patient outcomes, and guides healthcare decisions.

Q4: What is the purpose of internship in nursing?

A: To gain real-world clinical experience, apply knowledge, and develop professional skills under supervision.

Q5: Give one example of community health advanced concept.

A: Planning and implementing a vaccination campaign in the community.
